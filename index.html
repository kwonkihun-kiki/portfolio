    Bio

    Kihun Kwon is a graphic designer based in Seoul. His practice spans editorial, identity, and printed matter, with an interest in how typography, image, and material together hold a story.

    He works across self-initiated and commissioned projects, often collaborating with artists, small studios, and independent publishers.
